# Installing VSE

1\. Go to <https://visualstudio.microsoft.com/vs/express/> and download Community 2017. Open the .exe once the download completes. <img src="/rosco/product/mobileye/shield/1._installing_vse.png" class="align-center" width="400" />  
2. Click run. <img src="/rosco/product/mobileye/shield/2._installing_vse.png" class="align-center" width="400" />  
3. Click continue. <img src="/rosco/product/mobileye/shield/3._installing_vse.png" class="align-center" width="400" />  
4. The download and install process will begin. <img src="/rosco/product/mobileye/shield/4._installing_vse.png" class="align-center" width="400" />  
5. Check the box for **Desktop development with C++**. Under the Installation Details sidebar, scroll down and select **Visual C++ MFC for x86 and x 64** and **VC++ 2015.3 x 14.00 (x140) toolset for desktop**. Once these components are selected, click “Install” in the bottom right corner. <img src="/rosco/product/mobileye/shield/5._installing_vse.png" class="align-center" width="400" />  
6. The installation will continue until complete. You don’t need to create an account or run the program. After Visual Studio is installed on your computer, refer to the document [Updating Junction Box Software](/rosco/product/mobileye/shield/installation_guide/updating_junction_box_software). <img src="/rosco/product/mobileye/shield/6._installing_vse.png" class="align-center" width="400" />
