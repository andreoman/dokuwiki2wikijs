# Software

### DV-PRO 5 Player

#### Overview

DVPro5 video player software enabled [Dual-Vision® XC4](/user/product/dual-vision_recording/dual-vision_xc4/home) customers to access their video files and manage their data. With the player, customers can download videos and configuration settings from SD cards or the RoscoLive website. Video files can be watched, downloaded and converted from .NVR to .AVI video format for playback on popular video player software. DVXC4 configurations can be customized. SD cards can be reformatted and rid of any corrupt files for optimal performance. All of this and much more can be done through the DVPro5 player.
